About Our Organization

Our Organization exists to holistically and positively contribute to the wellbeing of the vulnerable and less privilaged, both girls and boys aged between 13 and 19, in the areas of education and health.

View Company Profile

Our Programmes

The organization programs focus on health and livelihood improvement. These thematic areas incorporate; Nutrition, Comprehensive Mental Health, Safe Space and other health related diseases (HIV, T.B and Malaria).

Nutrition

Nutrition activities present a key link to holistic approaches towards addressing Mental Health Challenges at primary, secondary and tertiary level of health service provision

  • Community Nutrition Awareness to the Caregivers of the beneficiaries
  • Feeding the school going children

Mental Health

Mental Health remains the core undertaking with comprehensive commitment on prevention, treatment, drug supply chain, training, diagnostic equipment and advocacy.

Safe - Space

Safe Space programs are designed for continuum support to health and development challenges faced by adolescents and youths particularly, sensitive on girls and young women.
